Size guide
A bracelet should sit snugly on the wrist and not slide up and down.
To measure your bracelet size, hold out your hand with your thumb outstretched and aligned with your other fingers. Using a measuring tape or even a strip of paper, measure your hand circumference.

Recommended to wear 15 - 20 charms regardless of bracelet length.
